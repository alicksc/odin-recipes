Italian Schiacciata Sandwich Recipe

Description

A rustic Tuscan sandwich layered with Italian meats, creamy cheese, and truffle spread on crispy schiacciata bread, just like in Florence.

Ingredients

  • Ciabatta bread
  • Prosciutto
  • Spinach
  • Arugula
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive oil/Truffle oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the bread
    • Slice the ciabatta bread in half and drizzle both sides with desired oil.
  2. Add cheese and meat
    • Places slices of mozzarella and layer with proscuitto.
  3. Add vegetables and toppings
    • Layer fresh spinach and arugula on bottom half of the bread.
    • Add sun-dried tomatoes for flavor.
  4. Assemble and serve
    • Close the sandwich, press gently, and toast if desired!
